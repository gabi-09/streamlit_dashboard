# Sustainability Dashboard

**Air Pollution, Public Health, and the cost of Inaction
across Europe (2005-2023)**

The dashboard pulls together three datasets that are usually
examined in isolation, it lets you explore them side by side! 
The combined analysis page specifically it lets you see, for
any country, whether countries with higher pollution also pay
a higher mortality and financial price.

### Features

- *Home* includes KPI cards, headline trends, and a dataset 
overview.
- *Emissions* includes longterm trends in six air pollutants
with country and year range filtering.
- *Health Impact* shows the premature deaths and years of life
lost attributable to PM2.5
- *Expenditure* includes the health expenditure per capita, with
country rankings
- *Analysis* shows a bubble chart relating all three datasets in a 
single view, with optional OLS trendline and pairwise correlations.


All plotly visualisations use the Okabe-Ito palette for colourblind
accessibility.

## Data sources

Air pollutant emissions - European Environment Agency (EEA)

Premature deaths from PM2.5 - Eurostat

Health expenditure per capita - World Bank

The data used in the dashboard is restricted to the common window
where all three datasets have (most)data filled. Any countries with
18+ years of missing data were dropped. Sub-national figures for premature
deaths were aggregated to national totals.
